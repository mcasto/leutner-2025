I was captivated from the very first chapter. The life portrayed in "Race Consciousness" is almost unbelievable in its diversity and adventure, yet it's also marked by profound heartbreak and loss. What resonated with me most was the author's unwavering resilience; no matter the gravity of the challenges she faced, she always found a way to rise again. Her ability to continually reinvent herself is nothing short of inspiring.
— International Review of Books
