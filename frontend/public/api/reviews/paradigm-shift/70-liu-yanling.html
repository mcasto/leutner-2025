21st Century Paradigm Shift: What It Is and How to Thrive In Itraises the alarm about the problems, crisis, and danger facing the world today with a historical, scientific and modern view. Carol moves beyond a Euro-centric worldview and proposes new perspectives including those she learned from other cultures. By adopting advanced technologies, she justifies a envisions a revised paradigm of oneness, multi-dimensionality, and responsibility to create humanity's future. This book is thought-provoking, insightful, and enlightening.
